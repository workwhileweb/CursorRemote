## License Key

CursorRemote requires a license key to operate.

Enter your key via the Command Palette (`Ctrl+Shift+P` > **CursorRemote: Enter License Key**).

Your key is stored securely in the OS credential store — it never leaves your machine.

**Don't have a key?** Visit [cursor-remote.com/buy](https://cursor-remote.com/buy?utm_source=extension&utm_medium=walkthrough&utm_campaign=license) to get one.
