## Telegram Bot Integration

Get mobile push notifications and send commands to Cursor via a Telegram bot.

### What you'll need
1. A Telegram bot token from [@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather)
2. A Telegram supergroup with **Topics** enabled
3. Add the bot as an **admin** of the group

The **Setup Panel** walks you through each step — click **Set Up Telegram** above to begin.
